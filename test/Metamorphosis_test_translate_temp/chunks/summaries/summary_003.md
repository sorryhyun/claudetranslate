## Chunk 3 Summary

### Brief Summary
The family and chief clerk discover Gregor's transformed state when he opens the door using only his mouth and jaw, causing horrified reactions from all present. Gregor, despite his grotesque appearance, attempts to reassure the chief clerk of his professional reliability and work ethic, but the clerk flees in panic while Gregor pursues him, discovering his newfound physical control with his multiple legs, prompting his mother to cry out for help.

### Key Terms in This Section
- chief clerk: Authority figure from Gregor's workplace; witnesses the transformation and serves as crucial representative to the firm
- commercial traveller: Gregor's profession/identity; he emphasizes his devotion to this role despite his condition
- samples: Business materials (textile samples) Gregor claims to pack for work, though now impossible to carry
- locksmith: Professional summoned by the father, ultimately unnecessary as Gregor opens the door himself
- lieutenant: Military rank referenced in photograph on the wall, indicating Gregor's former status and respect
- vermin: The unnamed nature of what Gregor has become (deliberately ambiguous in original)
